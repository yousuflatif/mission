Get Involved

The question remains, what can you do? What can you, as ordinary, everyday citizens do to change this situation? The answer is simple, easy to remember, and sure to make you roll your eyes at the clichéd-ness of it. What you can do is get involved with the government. That means to vote at your elections, whether it is local, state, or federal. Once you do get in contact with your officials. Let them know this is the reason you elected them, and now you want them to produce results. Write letters to higher officials, start awareness campaigns, do whatever you can to make many people aware of this issue. You’ll think this is pointless and no one will care, and sometimes it will seem that way. But this is an issue where you have to get your hands dirty to keep this country safe, clean, and beautiful.
